ROCKET 1000

Buckle up for an exhilarating ride like no other! UNLEASH THE BEAST.

In order to give the supercar that extravagant BRABUS bold look, the BRABUS designers developed the thrillingly styled BRABUS ROCKET 1000 widebody for the four-door coupe. The components are manufactured from high-strength, yet lightweight carbon. The front features new wider fenders with integrated sweeping flares. At the rear axle, carbon flares add 7.8 centimeters (3.1 in) to the overall width of the GT. Of course, this conversion also includes tailor-made fender liners made from Kevlar.

Crafted for the true connoisseur of performance and style, the Rocket 1000 embodies a symphony of innovation and precision engineering. Every curve and contour has been meticulously sculpted to enhance aerodynamics and optimize performance, culminating in a design that not only turns heads but also slices through the air with effortless grace. From its sleek, aerodynamic silhouette to its meticulously crafted carbon fiber components, every aspect of the Rocket 1000 exudes elegance and sophistication. Step inside and experience luxury redefined, with an opulent interior that blends cutting-edge technology with unparalleled comfort. Whether tearing up the track or cruising the streets, the Rocket 1000 delivers an unrivaled driving experience that transcends ordinary boundaries.
