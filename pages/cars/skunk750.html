CONQUER THE STREETS.

Unleash your passion for driving with the Skunk 750, where elegance meets performance in perfect harmony. From its sleek, aerodynamic silhouette to its meticulously crafted interior, every detail of the Skunk 750 is designed to elevate your driving experience. Feel the wind in your hair as you command the road with confidence, knowing that the Skunk 750's maximum speed of 315 km/h is ready to take you wherever your heart desires. Embrace the thrill of open-air driving like never before with the Skunk 750.

The Skunk 750 is the ultimate display of modern performance engineering. Equipped with a 4.0-liter V8 engine equipped with two Skunk high-performance turbochargers, the two-door super-roadster sprints from 0 - 100 km/h in just 3.3 seconds. Its top speed is electronically limited at 315 km/h to protect the Continental SportContact 7 performance tires. 900 Nm of lightning-fast torque are unleashed instantly courtesy of its nine-speed automatic transmission, which can be shifted either automatically or manually via the paddle shifters at the steering wheel - all at the driver's leisure.
